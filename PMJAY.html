Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yogana

PMJAY provides a defined benefit cover of Rs. 5 lakh per family per year. This cover will take care of almost all secondary care and most of tertiary care procedures.The benefit cover will also include pre and post-hospitalisation expenses. All pre-existing conditions will be covered from day one of the policy. A defined transport allowance per hospitalization will also be paid to the beneficiary.

  • To ensure that nobody is left out (especially women, children and elderly) there will be no cap on family size and age in the scheme.
  • The beneficiaries can avail benefits in both public and empanelled private facilities.
  • This will lead to increased access to quality health and medication.

Related Questions

What is the Aim of PMJAY?

AB-PMJAY will target about 10.74 crore poor, deprived rural families and identified occupational category of urban workers' families as per the latest Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) data covering both rural and urban. The scheme is designed to be dynamic and aspirational and it would take into account any future changes in the exclusion/ inclusion/ deprivation/ occupational criteria in the SECC data.

What are the Eligibility criteria of This Missio?

1. Families having only one room with kucha walls and kucha roof
2. Female headed households with no adult male member between age 16 to 59.
3. Disabled member and no able bodied adult member in the family.
4. SC/ST households.
5. Landless households deriving major part of their income from manual casual labour.
6. Families in rural areas having any one of the following: households without shelter, destitute, living on alms, manual scavenger families, primitive tribal groups, legally released bonded labour.
7. For urban areas, 11 defined occupational categories are entitled under the scheme - Occupational Categories of Workers, Rag picker, Beggar, Domestic worker, Street vendor/ Cobbler/hawker / Other service provider working on streets, Construction worker/ Plumber/ Mason/ Labour/ Painter/ Welder/ Security guard/, Coolie and another head-load worker, Sweeper/ Sanitation worker / Mali, Home-based worker/ Artisan/ Handicrafts worker / Tailor, Transport worker/ Driver/ Conductor/ Helper to drivers and conductors/ Cart puller/ Rickshaw puller, Shop worker/ Assistant/ Peon in small establishment/ Helper/Delivery assistant / Attendant/ Waiter, Electrician/ Mechanic/ Assembler/ Repair worker, Washerman/ Chowkidar.

What are the Beneficiaries that are automatically Excluded?

1. Households having motorized 2/3/4 wheeler/fishing boat
2. Households having mechanized 3/4 wheeler agricultural equipment
3. Households having Kisan Credit Card with credit limit above Rs. 50,000
4. Households with non - agricultural enterprises registered with government
5. Any member of household earning more than Rs. 10,000/ - per month
6. House with three or more rooms with pucca walls and roof
7. Owns more than 2.5 acres of irrigated land with 1 irrigation equipment
8. Owns 5 acres or more of irrigated land for two or more crop season
9. Owning at least 7.5 acres of land or more with a t least one irrigation equipment
